Frequently Asked Questions

Bootlegga offers a wide range of shipping services, including domestic shipping within Ghana, international shipping to West African countries, express delivery options, and specialized handling for fragile or sensitive items.

Bootlegga provides real-time tracking for all shipments. Once your shipment is processed, you will receive a unique tracking number that you can use to monitor the progress of your shipment on our website or by contacting our customer support team.

Bootlegga accepts various payment methods, including credit cards, mobile money transfers, and bank transfers. You can securely make payments through our online platform or at any of our designated payment points across Ghana.

Shipping times vary depending on the destination and the type of service you choose. For domestic shipping within Ghana, delivery typically takes 1-3 business days. International shipping to West African countries may take 3-7 business days. Express delivery options are available for time-sensitive shipments.

Bootlegga offers insurance options to protect your shipments against loss, damage, or theft. Our insurance policy covers the declared value of your shipment, providing you with peace of mind. You can opt for insurance during the shipping process, and our team will assist you with any necessary claims.
